                               Three Switches, One Bulb
Task:
Devise a strategy to determine which of the three switches controls a bulb in another room, entering the room only once.

Step 1: switch on one of the lights for some time. Take it A
Step 2:switch on another light .Take it as B
Step 3: switch off the other(A)
Step 4: enter the room
Step 5: touch and feel the turned off lights. Notice which one is hot.
Findings: 
1: The turned on light's corresponding switch will be B.
2: The hot bulb will have the switch marked as A.                                             Since current causes heating effect.
3: The other turned off bulb will have the 3rd , unmarked switch.
